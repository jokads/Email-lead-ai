# JokaLead AI

**AI-powered lead research and Gmail draft assistant for agencies, freelancers and small businesses.**

JokaLead AI helps businesses discover public leads, analyze websites, find public contact emails and create multilingual outreach drafts with human review before sending.

Built for digital agencies, web designers, freelancers and local business growth.

## What it does

* Finds local businesses by country and niche
* Scans public websites for business emails
* Detects businesses with or without websites
* Creates Gmail drafts instead of sending automatically
* Supports multilingual outreach
* Uses AI to generate safe and professional draft messages
* Keeps human review before any email is sent
* Exports lead reports
* Helps agencies save time when contacting potential clients
